Welcome to MCK

interview practice buddy

Mock has been developed to help you prepare and gain confidence for interviews. You can generate questions, save them, and even mock interview yourself.

Practicing for interviews can be awkward and uncomfortable, but is vitally important to making a good impression. With Mock, you can practice in the comfort and privacy of your own home.

To get started, simply move some questions from the common quetions tab into your saved quetions and press play! You can also add custom questions directly to your saved questions or generate questions and select some to save.

Mock works better if it has some information about you and the job you're applying for so please fill in the details section at the end of this form.
